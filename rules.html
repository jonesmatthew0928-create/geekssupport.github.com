Geeks Community Rules

  1. Respect everyone
    No harassment, hate, bullying, slurs, or explicit content.
  2. No spam or disruption
    Don’t spam, troll, raid, or intentionally cause problems.
  3. Use the right channels
    Keep conversations relevant and in proper channels.
  4. Post responsibly
    No private info, rumors, or unsafe content.
  5. No advertising
    No self-promo or server ads without staff approval.
  6. Appropriate profiles
    Clean usernames and avatars only.
  7. Listen to staff
    Moderator and developer decisions are final.
  8. No exploiting rules
    Bending rules still counts as breaking them.
  9. No soundboards
    Soundboards = instant 60s timeout.
  10. No sexual talk
    No NSFW or sexual discussion.
  11. Let mods do their job
    Don’t leave or joke during moderation.
  12. Don’t ping owners
    They’re busy or offline.
  13. Don’t disrespect devs/owners
    No backtalk or insults.
